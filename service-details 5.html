1 Day - Negombo

Negombo is a vibrant coastal city known for its sandy beaches, bustling fish markets, and colonial architecture.

Things to Do:

  1. Visit Negombo Beach
  2. Dutch Canal Boat Ride
  3. Visit Negombo Fish Market (Lellama)
  4. Explore Negombo Night Market
  5. Visit St. Mary's Church
  6. Visit Angurukaramulla Temple

2 Day - Anuradapura

Anuradhapura is one of the ancient capitals of Sri Lanka, renowned for its well-preserved ruins of ancient Sri Lankan civilization.

Things to Do:

  1. Visit Sri Maha Bodhi Tree
  2. Sightseeing The Ancient ruined City
  3. Visit Isurumuniya Temple
  4. Go on a Jeep Safari In Wilpattu National Park
  5. Visit Mihintale
  6. Visit Avukana Buddha Statue

3 Day - Polonnaruwa

Polonnaruwa, another ancient capital, is famous for its archaeological sites showcasing the grandeur of medieval Sri Lankan architecture.

Things to Do:

  1. Explore the Ancient City of Polonnaruwa
  2. See the Gal Vihara Buddha Statues
  3. Visit the Royal Palace of King Parakramabahu
  4. Explore the Quadrangle (Sacred Complex)
  5. Discover the Parakrama Samudra (Sea of Parakrama)
  6. Visit the Archaeological Museum
  7. Go on a Wildlife Safari at Minneriya or Kaudulla National Park

4 Day - Trincomalee

Trincomalee is a port city known for its natural harbor, beautiful beaches, and the historic Koneswaram Temple.

Things to Do:

  1. Relax at Nilaveli Beach
  2. Visit Pigeon Island National Park
  3. Explore Fort Frederick
  4. Visit Koneswaram Temple
  5. Spot Blue Whales & Dolphins
  6. Swim at Marble Beach
  7. Explore the Hot Water Springs at Kanniya
  8. Watch the Sunset at Dutch Bay

5,6 Day - Sigiriya

Sigiriya, also known as Lion Rock, is an ancient rock fortress and palace ruin, offering panoramic views and intricate frescoes.

Things to Do:

  1. Climb Sigiriya Rock Fortress
  2. Climb Pidurangala Rock
  3. Go on a Village Safari
  4. Go on a jeep Safari on Minneriya / Kaudulla National Park
  5. Ayurvedic massage treatments

7 Day - Kandy

Kandy is a cultural hub, home to the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic and the picturesque Kandy Lake.

Things to Do:

  1. Visit Temple of the Tooth Relic
  2. Visit Peradeniya Botanical Gardens
  3. Watch Kandyan Cultural Dance Show
  4. Visit Ambuluwawa Tower
  5. Visit Herbal Spice Gardens

8,9 Day - Ella

Ella is a small town surrounded by lush tea plantations, offering scenic views, hiking trails, and attractions like the Nine Arches Bridge.

Things to Do:

  1. Hike to Little Adam's Peak
  2. Visit Nine Arches Bridge
  3. Take the Train Ride
  4. Explore Ravana Falls
  5. Hike to Ella Rock
  6. Go Zip-lining at Flying Ravana
  7. Enjoy Night-lift in Ella

10 Day - Mirissa

Mirissa is famed for its stunning beaches and as a prime spot for whale watching.

Things to Do:

  1. Go Whale Watching
  2. Relax at Mirissa Beach
  3. Climb to Coconut Tree Hill
  4. Surfing at Weligama Beach
  5. Visit Secret Beach (Hidden Gem)
  6. Explore Parrot Rock
  7. Take a Catamaran Sailing Tour

11 Day - Benthota

Bentota is a resort town known for its expansive beaches, water sports, and the serene Bentota River.

Things to Do:

  1. Relax at Bentota Beach
  2. Go on a Boat Safari in Madu River
  3. Do Water Sports at Bentota River
  4. Visit Kande Viharaya
  5. Visit Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery
  6. Do Snorkeling & Diving
  7. Do River Kayaking

12 Day - Colombo

Colombo is the bustling commercial capital of Sri Lanka, offering a mix of modern urban life and colonial charm.

Things to Do:

  1. Visit Gangaramaya Temple
  2. Visit the Red Mosque (Jami Ul-Alfar Mosque)
  3. Visit Lotus Tower
  4. Explore the Colombo National Museum
  5. Visit the Pettah Market
  6. Explore Dutch Hospital Shopping Precinct
  7. Experience Colombo's Nightlife
  8. Walk Along Galle Face Green
