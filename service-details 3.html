1 Day - Negombo

Negombo is a vibrant coastal city known for its sandy beaches, bustling fish markets, and colonial architecture.

Things to Do:

  1. Visit Negombo Beach
  2. Dutch Canal Boat Ride
  3. Visit Negombo Fish Market (Lellama)
  4. Explore Negombo Night Market
  5. Visit St. Mary's Church
  6. Visit Angurukaramulla Temple

2 Day - Sigiriya

Sigiriya, also known as Lion Rock, is an ancient rock fortress and palace ruin, offering panoramic views and intricate frescoes.

Things to Do:

  1. Climb Sigiriya Rock Fortress
  2. Climb Pidurangala Rock
  3. Go on a Village Safari
  4. Go on a jeep Safari on Minneriya / Kaudulla National Park
  5. Ayurvedic massage treatments

3 Day - Dambulla

Dambulla is renowned for its cave temple complex, the largest and best-preserved in Sri Lanka, adorned with Buddhist mural paintings and statues.

Things to Do:

  1. Visit Dambulla Cave Temple (Golden Temple)
  2. visit Golden Buddha Statue
  3. Go on a Village Safari
  4. Go on a jeep Safari on Minneriya / Kaudulla National Park
  5. Visit Local Marcket (Dambulla Economic Center)

4 Day - Kandy

Kandy is a cultural hub, home to the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic and the picturesque Kandy Lake.

Things to Do:

  1. Visit Temple of the Tooth Relic
  2. Visit Peradeniya Botanical Gardens
  3. Watch Kandyan Cultural Dance Show
  4. Visit Ambuluwawa Tower
  5. Visit Herbal Spice Gardens

5,6 Day - Ella

Ella is a small town surrounded by lush tea plantations, offering scenic views, hiking trails, and attractions like the Nine Arches Bridge.

Things to Do:

  1. Hike to Little Adam's Peak
  2. Visit Nine Arches Bridge
  3. Take the Train Ride
  4. Explore Ravana Falls
  5. Hike to Ella Rock
  6. Go Zip-lining at Flying Ravana
  7. Enjoy Night-lift in Ella

7 Day - Mirissa

Mirissa is famed for its stunning beaches and as a prime spot for whale watching.

Things to Do:

  1. Go Whale Watching
  2. Relax at Mirissa Beach
  3. Climb to Coconut Tree Hill
  4. Surfing at Weligama Beach
  5. Visit Secret Beach (Hidden Gem)
  6. Explore Parrot Rock
  7. Take a Catamaran Sailing Tour

8 Day - Galle

Galle is a historic city featuring the UNESCO-listed Galle Fort, showcasing Dutch colonial architecture.

Things to Do:

  1. Explore Galle Fort
  2. Visit Galle Lighthouse
  3. Relax at Unawatuna Beach
  4. Explore Jungle Beach
  5. Visit Sea Turtle Hatchery
  6. Scuba Diving & Snorkeling
